## 2. GIẢI PHÁP ĐỀ XUẤT / Proposed Solution

### Dashboard Báo Cáo Tự Động / Automated Reporting Dashboard

Mình sẽ xây dựng một dashboard web hiển thị tất cả số liệu kinh doanh theo thời gian thực, với báo cáo tự động gửi vào Zalo mỗi ngày.

---

**Component 1: Real-Time Sales Dashboard**
- Live web dashboard accessible from any device (phone, tablet, laptop)
- Key metrics: revenue today/this week/this month, order count, average order value
- Top-selling products with trend charts
- Revenue comparison: today vs. yesterday, this week vs. last week

**Component 2: Profit & Cost Analysis**
- Calculates gross profit per order (revenue minus platform fees, shipping, cost of goods)
- Tracks platform commission fees and advertising spend
- Monthly P&L breakdown by product category

**Component 3: Automated Daily/Weekly Reports**
- Daily summary sent to Zalo at 9am:
  - Yesterday's revenue, order count, top 5 products
  - Comparison to previous day and same day last week
- Weekly summary sent every Monday:
  - Week-over-week growth rate
  - Best and worst performing categories
  - Customer acquisition trends

**Component 4: Custom Alerts & Insights**
- Alert when daily revenue drops below target threshold
- Alert when a product's sales spike (to trigger restocking)
- Monthly automatic report generation (PDF) for bookkeeping
